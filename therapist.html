Massage Therapist

Gazelle Baumgard

Gazelle found her calling in the healing arts at a young age, her nurturing spirit and desire to help people has grown along with her through the years. Graduating from Sarasota School of Massage Therapy in 2003, she has been a licensed massage therapist for nearly 20 years. With experience in both medical and spa settings, she caters to her clients needs on an individual basis. She’s certified in many massage modalities including Swedish, Therapeutic, Medical, Shiatsu, Reflexology, Sports Therapy, Thai, Whole Body Integration, Myofascial Release, Pregnancy Massage, Hot Stone Massage, Lymphatic drainage and Cranial Sacral Therapy; specializing in Neuromuscular and Deep Tissue Massage. Gazelle is committed to helping people heal, she cares about what she does and you can feel it.

Tammie Friday

Tammie was born in Birmingham, Al. and after working several years in Atlanta, Ga. has put roots down in Sarasota, Fl. She graduated from Space Coast Health Institute in Cocoa Beach 29 years ago. Tammie has a professional attitude and a servant’s heart; always looking out for the patient’s best interest. They’re always impressed with her knowledge of the anatomy and physiology of the body. She is thorough, engaging and makes you feel as if you are the only person in her world. Always attentive to your every need and has an innate ability to find the trouble spots and alleviate them. Tammie loves what she does and it shows. Her talents include neuromuscular, deep tissue, therapeutic, and swedish massage therapy. She is also trained in reflexology, kinesio taping, cupping, guasha, myofascial release and trigger point work.
